Why El Paso?

El Paso is a friendly, convenient place for warehouses and businesses that move goods. The city’s location and community make operations smooth and cost‑effective.

Location & Transport

  • Situated on the U.S.–Mexico border, making cross‑border shipping simple and fast.
  • Interstate 10 runs through town, providing a straight shot east or west across the Southwest.
  • A nearby Foreign Trade Zone offers tax benefits for companies dealing in international goods.

Community & Infrastructure

  • Next door to an international airport with a large cargo facility for air shipments.
  • Plenty of industrial parks, distribution centers and rail lines to keep your supply chain moving.
  • A growing workforce and supportive business environment that makes it easy to find help and resources.

Tenant Services

Applications and rent payments are handled through RentSpree.

How to Apply

  • Begin by completing the RentSpree screening application. The fee is $39.99 per applicant and includes a TransUnion credit report, criminal background check and eviction history.
  • After submitting your screening, wait for review and approval.
  • Once approved, follow the RentSpree instructions to create your profile and move forward with leasing.
Apply Now

How to Pay Rent

  • Log in to your RentSpree account and open your dashboard.
  • Click the Rent Payment tab.
  • Select Pay Now for an immediate payment or Schedule Payment for a future payment.
  • Choose your payment method:
    Bank Account (ACH): Free
    Credit/Debit Card: 3% processing fee, up to $5,000 per transaction
  • If your bank does not appear, manual verification may be required and can take a few days.
  • Review the terms and submit your payment.
Auto Pay: If required under your lease, use the Schedule Payment option in your RentSpree profile to set up recurring payments.
