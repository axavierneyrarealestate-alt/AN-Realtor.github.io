Tenant Services

Applications and rent payments are handled through RentSpree.

How to Apply

To apply, complete the RentSpree screening process first. Screening fees typically cost $39.99 per applicant and may include a TransUnion credit report, criminal background check, and eviction history.

Once your application has been reviewed and approved, continue following the RentSpree steps to create your account and move forward with leasing.

Application

How to Pay Rent

  • Log in to your RentSpree account and open your dashboard.
  • Click the Rent Payment tab.
  • Select Pay Now for an immediate payment or Schedule Payment for a future payment.
  • Choose your payment method:
    Bank Account (ACH): Free
    Credit/Debit Card: 3% processing fee, up to $5,000 per transaction
  • If your bank does not appear, manual verification may be required and can take a few days.
  • Review the terms and submit your payment.
Auto Pay: If required under your lease, use the Schedule Payment option in your RentSpree profile to set up recurring payments.

How Repair Requests Work

After submitting your request, the landlord or an authorized contact may reach out to schedule an appropriate time to review or complete the repair.

Important Notice:
Submitting a repair request does not guarantee that the requested item is the responsibility of the landlord or that the repair will be approved. Repair responsibilities can vary by property and by lease agreement. Please review your lease for details regarding covered repairs and tenant obligations.
